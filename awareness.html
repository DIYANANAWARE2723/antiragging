What is Ragging?

Ragging is any act that causes physical or psychological harm, embarrassment, or fear among students. It is strictly prohibited by law and college rules.

What is Women Harassment?

Women harassment includes any unwanted behavior, comments, physical contact, or actions that violate a woman's dignity and safety.

Do’s & Don’ts

  • ✔ Respect all students equally.
  • ✔ Report incidents immediately.
  • ❌ Do not tolerate abusive behavior.
  • ❌ Do not remain silent if you witness harassment.
